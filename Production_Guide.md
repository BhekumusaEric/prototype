# RouteMate – Production Guide
## How to Approach This Promo/Advert

---

## 1. VIDEO FORMAT OPTIONS

### Option A: Live Action (Recommended)
**Best for:** Emotional impact, authenticity, brand trust
- Cast 4 actors (diverse, relatable)
- Film in real vehicles on real roads
- Use split-screen editing
- Budget: Medium to High

### Option B: Animated/Motion Graphics
**Best for:** Quick turnaround, lower budget
- Character illustrations
- App interface animations
- Voiceover narration
- Budget: Low to Medium

### Option C: Hybrid (Best of Both)
**Best for:** Maximum impact
- Real footage + animated app overlays
- Live actors + motion graphics for app features
- Budget: Medium

### Script Finalization
- [x] Dialogue written
- [ ] Timing confirmed (1.5–2 min)

- 4 actors (Sylvia, Miriam, John, Ron)

- Vehicle props (steering wheels, dashboards)

- Checkers voucher (prop or digital)

---

## 3. PRODUCTION APPROACH

### Filming Style
**Tone:** Authentic, warm, hopeful (not corporate)
**Lighting:** Natural, golden hour for road scenes
**Camera:** Handheld for intimacy, stabilized for driving shots

### Shot List
1. **Establishing shots:** Empty roads, sunset, traffic
2. **Close-ups:** Faces showing emotion (concern, relief, joy)
3. **App interface:** Screen recordings of RouteMate features
4. **Flashback:** Sylvia's emergency (darker, tense)
5. **Community moment:** John arriving to help
6. **Reward reveal:** Phone notification, Sylvia's smile

### Audio
- Clear dialogue (lavalier mics for each actor)
- Ambient road sounds (subtle)
- Notification sounds (RouteMate ping)
- Background music (builds from tense to uplifting)

---

## 4. POST-PRODUCTION

### Editing
- Split-screen layout (4 drivers)
- Smooth transitions to flashbacks
- App interface overlays (After Effects)
- Color grading (warm, community feel)

### Graphics
- RouteMate logo animation
- Feature callouts (AI Diagnostics, Buddy Radio, etc.)
- Text overlays (tagline, website)

### Sound Design
- Music: Afro-fusion or contemporary South African sound
- SFX: Car sounds, phone pings, ambient traffic
- Voiceover: Optional narrator for tagline

### Primary Platforms
- **YouTube:** Full 2-minute version

---

## 6. BUDGET BREAKDOWN (Estimate)

### Low Budget (R15,000–R30,000)
- Amateur actors or real drivers
- Single-day shoot
- Minimal crew (2–3 people)
- Basic editing

### Medium Budget (R50,000–R100,000)
- Professional actors
- 2-day shoot
- Full crew (director, DP, sound, editor)
- Motion graphics

### High Budget (R150,000+)
- Known actors
- Multiple locations
- Full production team
- High-end post-production

## 7. ALTERNATIVE: DIY APPROACH

### For Gemini/AI Video Generation
1. **Feed the script** to Gemini or Runway ML
2. **Generate scenes** with AI video tools
3. **Use voiceover** from text-to-speech or voice actors
4. **Edit together** in CapCut, Premiere, or DaVinci Resolve

### Tools
- **AI Video:** Runway ML, Pika, Synthesia
- **Voiceover:** ElevenLabs, Descript
- **Editing:** CapCut (free), DaVinci Resolve (free)
- **Motion Graphics:** Canva Pro, After Effects
