# Gemini AI Video Generation Prompt
## RouteMate "4 Cousins on the Road" Promo

---

## MASTER PROMPT FOR GEMINI

Create a 1.5–2 minute promotional video for RouteMate, a fleet driver app that combines AI diagnostics, community features, and gamified rewards.

### Video Structure:

**Scene 1 (0:00–0:15): The Connection**
- Visual: Split-screen showing 4 diverse South African drivers in their vehicles (Sylvia, Miriam, John, Ron)
- Action: Phone notification sound. Drivers check their phones showing RouteMate app
- Dialogue: Sylvia discovers 3 nearby drivers all share the surname "Mathe" and jokes about finding her "cousins on the road"
- Tone: Light, friendly, surprising

**Scene 2 (0:15–0:25): The Reality**
- Visual: Transition to lonely South African highway at dusk, empty roads, potholes
- Dialogue: Miriam and Ron discuss how isolated and dangerous the roads can be
- Tone: Serious, establishing stakes

**Scene 3 (0:25–1:15): The Story**
- Visual: Focus on Sylvia. Flashback sequences showing her working long hours, exhausted, car breaking down on dark road
- Dialogue: Sylvia shares her story as a single mother who overworked to afford her daughter's school trip, had a medical emergency alone on the road, and used RouteMate's Buddy Radio to call for help
- Tone: Vulnerable, emotional, authentic

**Scene 4 (1:15–1:35): The Community**
- Visual: Return to split-screen. Show Miriam staying on the line, John driving to help with supplies
- Dialogue: Each driver explains how they responded to Sylvia's emergency through RouteMate
- Tone: Warm, community-focused, heroic

**Scene 5 (1:35–1:50): The Reward**
- Visual: Phone notification. RouteMate app shows "Community Engagement Bonus" with Checkers voucher
- Dialogue: Sylvia jokes about buying "4 Cousins" wine, others laugh and remind her "no drinking and driving," she invites them over after shift
- Tone: Joyful, playful, rewarding

**Scene 6 (1:50–2:00): The Tagline**
- Visual: All four drivers together (montage). RouteMate logo and app features appear
- Text: "RouteMate turns drivers from exposed individuals into prepared professionals"
- Features shown: AI Diagnostics, Buddy Radio, Route Optimization, Community Rewards
- End with: "Drive Connected. Drive Protected. Drive Rewarded."

### Visual Style:
- Authentic South African settings (highways, rural roads, townships)
- Warm color grading (golden hour, community feel)
- Split-screen for multiple drivers
- App interface overlays (modern, clean UI)
- Diverse casting (Black, Coloured, White, Indian South Africans)
- Natural lighting, realistic scenarios

### Audio:
- Clear dialogue with South African accents
- Subtle road ambient sounds
- Phone notification sounds
- Uplifting background music (Afro-fusion or contemporary)
- Emotional music swell during rescue story

### Key Themes:
- Loneliness → Community
- Danger → Safety
- Exploitation → Recognition
- Isolation → Connection

1. **Buddy Radio:** Chat interface showing nearby drivers
2. **Emergency Alert:** Red notification with location sharing

4. **Driver Map:** Shows 4 drivers with "Mathe" surname nearby
5. **AI Diagnostics Dashboard:** Vehicle health indicators
6. **Route Optimization:** Map with optimized delivery route
