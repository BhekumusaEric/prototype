## COLOR PALETTE

**Opening/Community Scenes:**
- Warm oranges (#FF8C42)
- Golden yellows (#FFD700)
- Friendly blues (#4A90E2)

**Emergency/Flashback:**
- Muted grays (#6B6B6B)
- Dark blues (#2C3E50)
- Desaturated tones

**Reward/Celebration:**
- Bright greens (#2ECC71)
- Vibrant purples (#9B59B6)
- Energetic yellows (#F1C40F)
