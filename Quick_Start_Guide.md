# RouteMate Promo – Quick Start Guide
## Get Your Video Made in 3 Steps

---

## 🎬 STEP 1: CHOOSE YOUR APPROACH

### A. AI Video Generation (Fastest, Lowest Cost)
**Time:** 1–3 days  
**Cost:** R0–R5,000  
**Tools:** Gemini, Runway ML, Synthesia, ElevenLabs

**Action:**
1. Copy the script from `Gemini_Video_Prompt.md`
2. Paste into Gemini or Runway ML
3. Generate scene-by-scene
4. Edit together in CapCut or DaVinci Resolve

---

### B. Live Action (Most Authentic)
**Time:** 1–2 weeks  
**Cost:** R30,000–R100,000  
**Tools:** Camera, actors, editing software

**Action:**
1. Cast 4 actors (post on Casting Network SA or Backstage)
2. Scout locations (highway, vehicle interiors)
3. Film over 1–2 days
4. Edit in Premiere Pro or Final Cut

---

### C. Hybrid (Best Balance)
**Time:** 1 week  
**Cost:** R15,000–R50,000  
**Tools:** Stock footage + AI + motion graphics

**Action:**
1. Use stock footage for road scenes (Pexels, Unsplash)
2. Generate character scenes with AI
3. Create app interface in After Effects or Canva
4. Combine in editing software

---

## 🎯 STEP 2: PRODUCTION WORKFLOW

### Week 1: Pre-Production
- [ ] Finalize script (use `RouteMate_Promo_Script.md`)
- [ ] Create storyboard (use `Storyboard.md`)
- [ ] Cast actors OR prepare AI prompts
- [ ] Scout locations OR source stock footage
- [ ] Design app interface mockups

### Week 2: Production
- [ ] Film scenes OR generate AI videos
- [ ] Record voiceovers (actors or ElevenLabs)
- [ ] Capture app interface screen recordings
- [ ] Gather B-roll (roads, vehicles, South African landscapes)

### Week 3: Post-Production
- [ ] Edit scenes together (follow storyboard timing)
- [ ] Add music (Epidemic Sound, Artlist)
- [ ] Color grade (warm, community feel)
- [ ] Add graphics (logo, tagline, features)
- [ ] Mix audio (dialogue, music, SFX)
- [ ] Export in multiple formats (16:9, 9:16, 1:1)

## 💡 PRO TIPS

### For AI Generation:
- Generate scenes separately, then edit together
- Use consistent character descriptions across prompts
- Upscale resolution with Topaz Video AI if needed
- Add human voiceovers for authenticity

### For Live Action:
- Film during golden hour (1 hour before sunset)
- Use lavalier mics for clear dialogue
- Get multiple takes of emotional scenes
- Capture extra B-roll for editing flexibility

### For Editing:
- Start with rough cut (timing and flow)
- Add music early to set emotional tone
- Use J-cuts and L-cuts for smooth transitions
- Export test versions and get feedback
