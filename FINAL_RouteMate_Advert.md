# RouteMate: The Power of Community (Enhanced Promo Script)

**Format:** 120-Second Cinematic Promo  
**Objective:** Showcase RouteMate’s "Community-First" and "Human-Centered" solution for Wesbank Fleet FML drivers.

---

## SCENE 1: THE DISCOVERY (0:00 - 0:20)

**VISUAL:** High-energy split-screen. Four drivers in their trucks, navigating different terrains: urban Joburg, misty Midlands, a dusty Northern Cape road, and a rainy Cape Town harbor.

**AUDIO:** Upbeat, modern Afro-beat with a subtle "Wesbank" sonic jingle at the intro.

**SYLVIA (SME Courier):** *(Checks her dashboard-mounted phone)*  
"Wait... RouteMate says there are three drivers within 5kms of my route. John, Miriam, and Ron? All 'Mathe'? Did I just find my cousins on the long haul?"

**JOHN (Construction Logistics):** *(Laughs into his headset)*  
"Consider us your road-family, Sylvia. Welcome to the Mathe Fleet!"

**VISUAL:** An overlay of the **RouteMate App** shows the "Buddy Radio" pulsing with 4 icons, highlighting a "Community Connection established" message.

---

## SCENE 2: THE HUMAN-CENTERED EDGE (0:20 - 0:45)

**VISUAL:** Sylvia is driving. The lighting shifts to a cooler, more clinical tone. The app interface on the screen shows a "Driver Health Monitor." A subtle red glow appears on the edge of the phone screen.

**NARRATOR (Voiceover, warm & professional):**  
"In the old days, you were alone with your fatigue. But at Wesbank Fleet FML, we believe the most important part of the vehicle... is the person behind the wheel."

**VISUAL:** Close-up on the app. It displays: *[FATIGUE ALERT: Voice patterns & steering micro-movements suggest high stress. Activating Buddy-Support.]*

**SYLVIA:** *(Sighs, looks visibly drained)*  
"It’s been a long week. I haven't seen my daughter since Tuesday. I just need to make this last delivery."

---

## SCENE 3: THE INTERVENTION (0:45 - 1:15)

**VISUAL:** Miriam’s screen pops up on Sylvia’s dashboard via "Buddy Radio."

**MIRIAM (Heavy Haulage):**  
"Sylvia? I see your alert, sis. You sound tired. Stay with me. Tell me about that school trip your daughter is planning. What’s her name again? Thandeka?"

**SYLVIA:** *(Eyes brighten slightly)*  
"Yeah... Thandeka. She wants to be a vet. I worked the extra shifts to pay for the excursion."

**VISUAL:** Transition to **AI Route Optimization** screen.  
**NARRATOR:**  
"While Miriam keeps the connection, RouteMate’s AI re-routes John, who’s already finished his SME delivery nearby."

**JOHN:** *(Voice comes through the radio)*  
"Don't worry about the fuel stop, Sylvia. I’m pulling over at the next Engen. I’ve got water, a sandwich, and I’m staying right here until the Wesbank Roadside Assist arrives for that tire check the AI flagged."

---

## SCENE 4: THE WESBANK PAYOFF (1:15 - 1:45)

**VISUAL:** Montage of the four drivers meeting briefly at a safe rest stop. They share a laugh. Sylvia looks recovered, holding a coffee John bought.

**SYLVIA:**  
"I didn't just get home safe. I got home *connected*."

**VISUAL:** Sylvia’s phone pings again. A vibrant animation: **[USER REWARD: Community Guardian Bonus - R500 Checkers/Shoprite Voucher Shared!]**

**SYLVIA:** *(To the others)*  
"Check your apps! Our team score just hit the 'Safety Milestone.' Thandeka’s excursion snacks are on Wesbank today!"

**RON (SME Owner/Driver):**  
"That’s the beauty of it. When my drivers are safe and happy, my business grows. We don't just drive for Wesbank; we drive with each other."

---

## SCENE 5: THE HOOK (1:45 - 2:00)

**VISUAL:** All four vehicles driving in an offset formation (like a flock) as the sun sets. The RouteMate and Wesbank Fleet FML logos lock together on screen.

**TEXT ON SCREEN:**  
**RouteMate**  
*Human Intelligence. Community Heart. Fleet Performance.*

**SYLVIA (V.O.):**  
"So, John... about that celebratory 4 Cousins wine later?"

**JOHN (V.O., laughing):**  
"Absolutely. But only *after* the keys are handed in!"

**FADE TO BLACK.**  
**TEXT:** *Powered by Wesbank Fleet FML. The Future of FML is Community.*
