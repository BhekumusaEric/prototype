# RouteMate – Promotional Video Script
## "4 Cousins on the Road"
**Duration:** 1.5–2 minutes

---

## VISUAL CONCEPT
- Split-screen showing 4 drivers in their vehicles
- RouteMate app interface overlays
- Real road footage (South African highways/rural roads)
- Warm, community-focused color grading

---

## SCENE 1: THE CONNECTION (0:00–0:15)
**VISUAL:** Four split screens. Each driver in their vehicle. Phone notification sound.

**SYLVIA** *(checking phone, surprised)*  
RouteMate says there are three drivers nearby… and all your surnames are Mathe? So I guess I found my cousins on the road.

**JOHN** *(smiling)*  
Welcome to the family.

**VISUAL:** App interface shows "Buddy Radio" feature with 4 connected drivers

---

## SCENE 2: THE REALITY (0:15–0:25)
**VISUAL:** Transition to lonely, empty road. Sunset/dusk lighting.

**MIRIAM**  
Good thing too. This road is far from help.

**RON**  
One problem out here becomes a big one very fast.

**VISUAL:** Quick cuts: pothole, broken traffic light, empty petrol station

---

## SCENE 3: THE STORY (0:25–1:15)
**VISUAL:** Focus on Sylvia. Flashback sequences in muted tones.

**SYLVIA** *(serious, vulnerable)*  
That's actually why I wanted to tell you something. RouteMate didn't just help my workday… it saved my life.

I'm a single mother. I was an orphan growing up, so with my daughter I try to give her the things I never had. She had a school trip coming up. I promised she would go.

**VISUAL:** Flashback – Sylvia working long hours, skipping stops, exhausted

So I worked a long shift. Too long. Skipped petrol stops. Skipped food. Didn't sleep much. I just wanted to finish one last delivery on a road just like this one.

Then my car shut down.

**VISUAL:** Car stopped on dark road. Sylvia dizzy, struggling.

And I felt dizzy. Like my body might shut down next.

I called an ambulance… but the nearest hospital was far. And I was alone.

**VISUAL:** Sylvia opens RouteMate app

Then I remembered the new software at work — RouteMate.

I logged in. Five drivers nearby finishing their last trips. I opened Buddy Radio and asked if anyone was there.

---

## SCENE 4: THE COMMUNITY (1:15–1:35)
**VISUAL:** Return to split screen. Miriam's face shows empathy.

**MIRIAM**  
I answered. I stayed on the line with her. Told her to breathe and stay awake. Called emergency services and shared her location.

**SYLVIA**  
Then another driver I'd chatted with earlier got the alert.

**JOHN**  
That was me. I saw the RouteMate ping and drove straight there with food, water, and fuel.

**VISUAL:** Flashback – John arriving, helping Sylvia

**RON**  
That's what drivers do. We look out for each other in ways people don't always see.

**SYLVIA**  
Exactly. Drivers turn strangers into community.

---

## SCENE 5: THE REWARD (1:35–1:50)
**VISUAL:** Phone notification. App shows reward animation.

**SYLVIA** *(checking phone, surprised smile)*  
Wait — RouteMate just gave me a reward: Community Engagement Bonus… a Checkers voucher.

That's enough to pay for my daughter's trip and snacks… and then some.

*(smiles)*  
Imagine if I used it to buy a bottle of 4 Cousins.

**MIRIAM** *(laughing)*  
Ey! No drinking and driving!

**SYLVIA**  
Relax — after the shift. I'll host all of you.

**RON**  
Now that sounds like real community.

---

## SCENE 6: THE TAGLINE (1:50–2:00)
**VISUAL:** All four drivers together (montage). RouteMate logo appears.

**ALL TOGETHER** *(voiceover)*  
RouteMate turns drivers from exposed individuals into prepared professionals.

**VISUAL:** App features flash on screen:
- AI Diagnostics
- Buddy Radio
- Route Optimization
- Community Rewards

**TEXT ON SCREEN:**  
**RouteMate**  
*Drive Connected. Drive Protected. Drive Rewarded.*

**FADE TO BLACK**

---

## PRODUCTION NOTES
- Authentic casting: Real drivers or actors who understand the role
- South African locations and accents
- Natural lighting for realism
- Subtle product placement (app interface)
- Emotional beats balanced with hope
- Music: Warm, uplifting, community-focused
